Lasagna

Description

Ingrediants

  • buns
  • ground beef
  • cheese
  • ketchup
  • mayonaise

Steps

  1. Make ground beef into patties
  2. Cook ground beef patties
  3. Put cheese on the patties
  4. Melt the cheese
  5. Put patties on bottom buns
  6. Put mayonaise and ketchup on top buns
  7. Put top buns on the patties
